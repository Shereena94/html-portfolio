The Best Movies According to Shereena

My favourite 3 movies

Magnificient Century

Magnificient Century is a turkish drama.
The series follows the reign of Suleiman the Magnificent,
the most renowned ruler of the Ottoman Empire.

The Lion King

The plot follows Simba, a young lion who must embrace his role as
the rightful king of his homeland following the murder of his father,
Mufasa, at the hands of his uncle, Scar.

Vikram

Vikram is a Tamil movie lead by the Legend Kamal Hassan.
Is a very thrilling movie.
